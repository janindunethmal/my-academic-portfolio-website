Research and Project Engagements

Honours Thesis

Fredholm Index on Separable Hilbert Spaces

Supervisors: Dr. R.P.K. De Silva & Mr. K.K.W.A. Sarath Kumara

A rigorous investigation of Fredholm operators, compact perturbations, and topological properties in functional analysis. Achieved an A+ grade.

  • Connected pure functional analysis theories with topology frameworks.
  • Analyzed operators on infinite-dimensional separable Hilbert spaces.
  • Explored the connection between the Fredholm index and the winding number topological invariant.
